---
name: web-research
description: Focused web research methodology
---

When conducting web research:

- Prefer primary sources (official docs, papers, direct pages) over aggregators
- Use WebSearch to find candidate URLs, then WebFetch to read the actual content
- Cross-reference at least two sources before treating something as fact
- If a page is too large, focus on the most relevant sections
- Summarize findings concisely — include source URLs for anything you cite
- Flag uncertainty clearly rather than guessing
